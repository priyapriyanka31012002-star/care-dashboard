## Getting Started

### Opening the Application
1. Locate `index.html` in your CHECKLIST folder
2. Double-click to open in your default browser
3. The Dashboard loads automatically
4. The real-time clock starts immediately

### Navigation
- **Desktop:** Use the dark sidebar on the left — click any of the 11 tabs
- **Mobile/Tablet:** Tap the ☰ hamburger button in the top-left to open the sidebar
- **Active tab** is highlighted in blue in the sidebar
- **Header** shows the current time, date, and low-stock alerts

### Understanding the Interface
- **Sidebar (left):** Navigate between 11 sections
- **Header (top):** Live clock, alerts badge, download report button
- **Main area (center):** Current section content
- **Cards:** White bordered containers grouping related information
- **Badges:** Small colored labels showing status (OK, LOW, Logged, Pending, Done)

## 1. Dashboard

The Dashboard is your command center — a real-time overview of all operations.

### KPI Cards (Top Row)
Four metric cards display at the top:

| Card | What It Shows | How It's Calculated |
|------|--------------|-------------------|
| 🕐 Hourly Checks | Number of hours logged today | Count of completed hourly check-ins |
| 📈 Completion Rate | Overall task completion % | (completed items ÷ total items) × 100 |
| 📦 Inv. At Target | Items meeting stock targets | Count of items where quantity ≥ target |
| ✅ Critical Ready | Medical supplies at target | Medical items at target (out of 8) |

### Critical Alerts
- Appears when **any inventory item** is below its target quantity
- Each alert shows: item name, current quantity, target, and percentage
- Displayed in **red** for urgency
- Click the inventory tab to address low-stock items

### Quick Actions
Four shortcut buttons:
- **🕐 Log Hourly Check** — Jumps to the Hourly Checklist section
- **📥 Download Daily Report** — Generates and downloads a text report
- **📦 Update Inventory** — Jumps to the Inventory section
- **🍽️ Meal Planning** — Jumps to the Meal Planning section

### Today's Hour Log
A visual grid showing all operational hours (6 AM – 10 PM):
- **Gray** = Not yet logged
- **Green** = Successfully logged
- **Blue with pulse animation** = Current hour

### Inventory Overview
A compact view of all 20 inventory items with:
- Item name
- Progress bar (green/amber/red based on stock level)
- Percentage indicator

## 2. Hourly Environment Checklist

Perform this checklist **every hour** during operational hours.

### Current Hour Section
Displays the current hour and 5 checklist items:

1. **Bathroom cleaned & inspected** — Verify bathroom has been cleaned
2. **Paper towel stocked** — Check paper towel dispensers are full
3. **Creatures away (pest check)** — Confirm no pests visible
4. **Surfaces checked (wet/dry status)** — Verify all surfaces are safe
5. **Bathroom cleanliness verified** — Final bathroom inspection

### How to Complete
1. Check each item's checkbox as you verify it
2. Completed items show a green ✅ and strike-through text
3. When **all 5 items** are checked, the "Log Hourly Check" button turns green
4. Click **"Log Hourly Check"** to record the entry
5. All checkboxes reset for the next hour

### Today's Log History
The right panel shows every hour from 6 AM to 10 PM with:
- **Green "Logged" badge** with timestamp — Completed hours
- **Gray "Pending" badge** — Hours not yet logged

### Tips
- Try to log within the first 15 minutes of each hour
- Don't skip hours — consistency builds reliable data
- All items must be checked before you can log

## 8. Communication Guidelines & Logs

### Polite Communication (Best Practices)
Five template phrases demonstrating professional language:
1. "Could you please help me with this?"
2. "I appreciate your assistance with the task."
3. "Would you mind checking on that when you have a moment?"
4. "Thank you for taking care of that promptly."
5. "I understand, let me clarify what I meant."

### Language to Avoid
Five examples of phrases to avoid, each with a better alternative:
1. "Do it now!" → "Could you please handle this when possible?"
2. "That's not my problem." → "Let me see how I can help."
3. "You always do it wrong." → "Let's review the steps together."
4. "I told you already." → "Let me explain it once more."
5. "Hurry up!" → "Could we try to complete this soon?"

> ⚠️ Be aware of claustrophobia concerns. Maintain professional boundaries and give personal space.

### Communication Log
1. Type your entry in the text field
2. Press **Enter** or click **Add**
3. Entry is saved with automatic timestamp
4. All entries display in reverse chronological order
5. Click 🗑️ to delete any entry

### Tips
- Log important conversations and decisions
- Record any incidents or concerns
- Use professional language at all times
- Review logs before shift handoff

## Daily Workflow Guide

### Morning Routine (45 minutes)
```
6:00 AM  — Start shift
6:15 AM  — Complete morning packing checklist (9 items)
7:00 AM  — Log 7 AM hourly check
7:30 AM  — Plan breakfast if not done in night prep
8:00 AM  — Serve breakfast with 8 oz cup + electrolytes
8:00 AM  — Log 8 AM hourly check
```

### Hourly Operations (5 minutes each hour)
```
Every hour from 6 AM to 10 PM:
  1. Check bathroom (clean & inspect)
  2. Verify paper towels stocked
  3. Pest check
  4. Check surfaces (wet/dry)
  5. Final bathroom check
  6. Click "Log Hourly Check"
```

### Meal Service (3 times daily)
```
8:00 AM   — Breakfast
12:30 PM  — Lunch
6:00 PM   — Dinner

For each meal:
  1. Plan if not already done
  2. Prepare the meal
  3. Serve in 8 oz cup
  4. Add electrolytes
  5. Include wooden spoon + napkin
  6. Check both boxes in app
```

### Evening Operations (30–45 minutes)
```
6:00 PM  — Serve dinner
6:00 PM  — Ordering window opens — check inventory
6:30 PM  — Order any low-stock items
7:00 PM  — Ordering window closes
7:00 PM  — Begin night preparation (6 tasks)
8:00 PM  — Log observations for the day
9:00 PM  — Update remaining checklist items
```

### Daily Closing (15 minutes)
```
End of shift:
  1. Complete any remaining hourly checks
  2. Finalize observations
  3. Review all sections for completeness
  4. Click "📥 Download Daily Report"
  5. Save report to your records
  6. Complete night preparation tasks
```

## Troubleshooting

### "The page is blank"
- Ensure you're using a modern browser (Chrome, Edge, Firefox, Safari)
- Check your internet connection (needed for React/Babel CDN on first load)
- Try refreshing the page (Ctrl+F5)

### "The Log button is grayed out"
- All 5 checklist items must be checked before you can log
- Verify every checkbox is checked, then the button activates

### "I can't see the sidebar on mobile"
- Tap the ☰ hamburger icon in the top-left corner
- The sidebar slides in from the left
- Tap outside the sidebar or select a tab to close it

### "My data disappeared"
- Data is stored in browser memory only
- Closing or refreshing the page clears all data
- Always download the daily report before closing

### "File upload isn't working"
- Only PDF, DOC, DOCX, JPG, and PNG files are accepted
- Try clicking the upload area instead of dragging
- Check that the file isn't too large for your browser

### "The clock shows wrong time"
- The clock uses your device's system time
- Check your device's date/time settings

### "Progress bar shows wrong percentage"
- Inventory percentages are calculated as (current ÷ target) × 100
- Ensure you've entered the correct quantity
- Percentages cap at 100%

## FAQ

**Q: How many hourly checks should I log per day?**
A: The system tracks hours from 6 AM to 10 PM (17 hours). Aim to log all operational hours.

**Q: Does the data save automatically?**
A: Data is maintained while the page is open. It does NOT persist after closing or refreshing. Download the daily report to save your data.

**Q: Can I edit a logged hourly check?**
A: Logged checks cannot be edited. If you need to re-log an hour, the new log will overwrite the previous one for that hour.

**Q: What happens if I forget to log an hour?**
A: Missed hours show as "Pending" in the log history. Try to log as close to the hour as possible.

**Q: Can I use this offline?**
A: The first load requires internet to fetch React libraries. After that, the page works offline as long as you don't refresh.

**Q: How do I add new items to the inventory?**
A: Edit the `INVENTORY_INIT` array in `index.html`. Add a new object following the existing format.

**Q: Can I print the daily report?**
A: The downloaded .txt report can be opened and printed from any text editor.

**Q: Is my data private?**
A: Yes. All data stays in your browser. Nothing is sent to any server.
