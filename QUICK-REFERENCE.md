## 💬 COMMUNICATION QUICK TIPS

### ✅ USE These Phrases
- "Could you please help me with this?"
- "I appreciate your assistance."
- "Would you mind checking on that?"
- "Thank you for handling that promptly."
- "Let me clarify what I meant."

### 🚫 AVOID These Phrases
- "Do it now!" → Say: "Could you handle this when possible?"
- "Not my problem." → Say: "Let me see how I can help."
- "You always do it wrong." → Say: "Let's review together."
- "I told you already." → Say: "Let me explain once more."
- "Hurry up!" → Say: "Could we try to finish soon?"
